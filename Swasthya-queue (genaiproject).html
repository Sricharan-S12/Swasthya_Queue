Patient journey
1
Patient registers (any channel)
Web form, USSD *599# on keypad phone, SMS/WhatsApp chatbot, or via ASHA worker. No smartphone needed for USSD/SMS. Works fully offline with local queue storage.
2
Symptom questionnaire
5–10 structured questions about symptoms, severity (1–5 scale), and duration. Multilingual — Hindi and English, plus 3 regional languages.
3
Auto triage & scoring
START-inspired algorithm scores each patient in under 1 second. Critical symptoms score +4, moderate +2, mild +1. Age multiplier applied for elderly/children.
4
Token issued, queue updated
Patient gets a queue token with estimated wait time. Doctor queue reorders automatically by severity score. Saved locally if offline, synced when reconnected.
5
RED patient escalation protocol
2 min: If no doctor response, alert backup doctor + ASHA worker via SMS. 5 min: Auto-trigger ambulance dispatch + referral to nearest hospital. Timer visible on queue card.
6
Referral & dispatch (if needed)
Doctor one-taps to reserve a bed, dispatch ambulance, and send auto-generated referral — simultaneously. Full status timeline visible to both doctor and hospital.
