Patient journey
1
Patient registers (any channel)
Web form, USSD *599# on keypad phone, SMS/WhatsApp chatbot, or via ASHA worker at clinic kiosk. No smartphone or internet needed for USSD/SMS.
2
Symptom questionnaire
5–10 structured questions about symptoms, severity (1–5 scale), and duration. Multilingual — Hindi and English.
3
Auto triage & scoring
START-inspired algorithm scores each patient in under 1 second. Critical symptoms score +4, moderate +2, mild +1. Age multiplier applied for elderly / children.
4
Token issued, queue updated
Patient gets a queue token with estimated wait time. Doctor queue reorders automatically by severity score. SMS / WhatsApp notification sent.
5
Doctor sees structured brief
Before the teleconsultation, doctor sees vitals, symptoms, triage score, and a 3-sentence AI brief. No need to ask basic questions again.
6
Referral & dispatch (if needed)
For critical patients: doctor one-taps to reserve a bed at the nearest suitable hospital, dispatch 108 ambulance, and send auto-generated referral notice — all simultaneously.
